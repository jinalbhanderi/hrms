Have you been vaccinated?

Vaccination provided much needed relief to the working places. Many employees registered themselves to get vaccinated and get back to the office. The employee's vaccination records have become a new set of HR documents.

Super HRMS has launched Vaccination Status module where company's HR Team would be able to stay updated with the vaccination details of their employees.

  1. Power to Employees:

    Employees can voluntarily updated their vaccination status by providing consent. The HR team can view vaccination data and ensure necessary precautions and safety measures for the office environment and increase company productivity. It also helps the HR team to plan employee roster in advance after knowing data.

  2. Customer Facing Sectors:

    Vaccination has become mandatory for customer facing sectors such as Delivery Logistics, Hospital, Bank, etc... HR team will be well prepared by knowing the vaccination status with this module feature.

  3. Employee Wellness:

    The company would like to run vaccination drive for their employees can easily do so by having vaccination information of the employees on their side.

  4. Vaccination Compliance:

    The company can stay compliant for vaccination compliance norms which might include mandatory vaccination record management.
